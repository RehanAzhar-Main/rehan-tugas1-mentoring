This is Samosa

India

Samosa is a fried or baked pastry with a savoury filling, such as spiced potatoes, onions, peas, lentils, macaroni, noodles, chicken, beef, lamb, or other vegetables. It may take many forms, including triangular, cone-shaped, or half-moon shapes.
